About Me

I am interested in mathematics and computer science and their applications to problem solving. Recently, I have become very interested in Artificial Intelligence and learning about the mathematics used in AI and machine learning. The areas that interest me most are graph theory, propositional logic, combinatorics, and optimization. I also like more 'pure' areas of mathematics like set theory, topology, and mathematical logic. My strengths include logical reasoning, problem solving, and analytical thinking. Aside from mathematics and its immediate applications, I also enjoy web development and am working towards getting the skills necessary to be a full-stack developer.
